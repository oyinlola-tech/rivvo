# Code of Conduct

This Code of Conduct applies to all spaces managed under this repository, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and any direct communication related to the project.

## Our Standards

We are committed to providing a professional, respectful, and harassment‑free environment for everyone. We expect all participants to:

- Be respectful and considerate in language and actions.
- Welcome constructive feedback and differing viewpoints.
- Focus on the project’s goals and maintain technical professionalism.
- Refrain from personal attacks, intimidation, and harassment.
- Respect privacy and confidentiality of sensitive or personal data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behavior is unacceptable:

- Harassment, threats, or discrimination of any kind.
- Insults, derogatory comments, or hostile language.
- Sharing private or sensitive information without permission.
- Spamming or disruptive behavior that hinders collaboration.
- Misuse of repository resources or unauthorized access attempts.

## Enforcement

Maintainers reserve the right to take appropriate action for any behavior deemed inappropriate, including:

- Requesting a change in behavior.
- Removing content or comments.
- Blocking or banning users from the project spaces.
